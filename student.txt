# Attributes for both student-mat.csv (Math course) and student-por.csv (Portuguese language course) datasets:
0 age - student's age (numeric: from 15 to 22)
1 address - student's home address type (0 is U, 1 is R)
2 famsize - family size (LE3 = 0, GE3 = 1)
3 Medu - mother's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education)
4 Fedu - father's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education)
5 traveltime - home to school travel time (numeric: 1 - <15 min., 2 - 15 to 30 min., 3 - 30 min. to 1 hour, or 4 - >1 hour)
6 studytime - weekly study time (numeric: 1 - <2 hours, 2 - 2 to 5 hours, 3 - 5 to 10 hours, or 4 - >10 hours)
7 failures - number of past class failures (numeric: n if 1<=n<3, else 4) 0 = No, 1 = Yes
8 schoolsup - extra educational support (binary: yes or no)
9 famsup - family educational support (binary: yes or no)
10 paid - extra paid classes within the course subject (Math or Portuguese) (binary: yes or no)
11 activities - extra-curricular activities (binary: yes or no)
12 nursery - attended nursery school (binary: yes or no)
13 higher - wants to take higher education (binary: yes or no)
14 internet - Internet access at home (binary: yes or no)
15 romantic - with a romantic relationship (binary: yes or no)
16 famrel - quality of family relationships (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - excellent)
17 freetime - free time after school (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
18 goout - going out with friends (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
19 Dalc - workday alcohol consumption (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
20 Walc - weekend alcohol consumption (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
21 health - current health status (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - very good)
22 absences - number of school absences (numeric: from 0 to 93)

# these grades are related with the course subject, Math or Portuguese:
23 G1 - first period grade (numeric: from 0 to 20)
24 G2 - second period grade (numeric: from 0 to 20)
25 G3 - final grade (numeric: from 0 to 20, output target)
